Selecting our Project

Each member of this project put forth some ideas. Once we had a collection of ideas, we narrowed it down to just a few, before voting on our final choice, which was a Corn Disease Identifier. You can learn more in our motives tab. 

Collecting Data

We used a Kaggle dataset to find images for diseased and healthy plants. Then, we labeled the images (we created 4,178 labels) to be used in training our model. More labeled images will be added as we continue developing the model.  

Training our Model

We used python to collect, prepare, and evaluate our data. We utilized YOLO, an object detection software, to do the final detections. We ran our labeled images through YOLO to train it to identify the different types of corn leaf diseases.

Testing our Model

Finally, we tested our model with the remaining data; by evaluating which images the model correctly and incorrectly identified, our program generated a confusion matrix to analyze our results. Then, we deployed the model.
