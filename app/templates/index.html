Our Motive

Our motivation was driven by a desire to help farmers determine the health of their plants. Traditionally, farming is an area where AI is not utilized, something we sought to change. Slowly, this idea cultivated into a machine learning model designed to specify the type of disease a corn plant has. The impact of this project is huge since farmers can now look into specific treatment options and make adjustments to their crops accordingly. Since corn disease's are hard to diffrentiate for the human eye, our algorithm makes the proccess mroe efficient and accurate.
