Our Motive

This AI was built to help identify types of corn disease. It is mainly intended to help farmers efficiently determine the health of their corn plants. Differentiating between types of diseases is extremely difficult without professional expertise, so our algorithm streamlines the process. It can be applied around the world and make farming more efficient. Once the type of disease is diagnosed, farmers can take proactive steps to prevent is spread and treat the infected crops. We chose this project because, traditionally, farming does not utilize much machine learning. Our project had an opportunity to make a profound impact and create a platform for us to continue growing.
